Got Questions?

What makes a password “strong”?

A strong password is at least 12 characters long and mixes upper/lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols to resist guessing and brute‑force attacks.

Can I control pronouncability?

Not yet—but coming soon! You’ll be able to toggle a “pronounceable” mode for memorable, word‑like passwords.

Is any data sent to a server?

No—everything runs locally in your browser. We don’t collect or store any of your generated passwords.
