About & Help

KwikPass.app is a free, one‑page tool for generating strong, random passwords with no signup, no distractions, and full mobile support.

How it works

  1. Set your desired length (4–128 characters).
  2. Toggle lowercase, UPPERCASE, numbers, and symbols.
  3. Click “Generate” and then “Copy” to copy it to your clipboard.

Why use KwikPass?

  • No tracking: Runs entirely in your browser.
  • Fast & responsive: Works on desktop and mobile.
  • Ad‑free experience: Ads only appear outside the tool UI.

Need help?

If something isn’t working or you have suggestions, please open an issue on our GitHub repo.
